
Ingredients
0.3 kg greek yoghurt
2.5 cups self raising flour
0.5 kg lamb mince
3 cups spinach
1 capsicum
0.25 kg feta
1 garlic
mint
1 lemon for wedges
1 tbs olive oil
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp paprika

Method
1. For the dough. Place yoghurt in a bowl. Stir in a pinch of salt.
2. Gradually add the flour, while mixing.
3. Knead for 3 mins. Then let stand for 30 mins.
4. Heat the oil in a frying pan. 
5. Cook the lamb mince & garlic. 
6. Add the capsicum, cumin and paprika. Cook for 3 - 4 mins
7. In a bowl, mix lamb mixture with spinach and feta
8. Divide dough into 5 even balls and roll flat
9. Place filling in the middle, then fold and seal edges
10. Brush both sides with oil and plan in large fry pan on medium heat
11. Cook for 2 - 3 mins or until golden.
12. Serve with lemon wedges
