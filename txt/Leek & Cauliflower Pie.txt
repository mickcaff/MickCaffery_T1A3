
Ingredients
0.5 kg fish
0.8 kg cauliflower
0.5 cup parmesan
1 cup mozzarella
1 tbs plain flour
0.5 cup cream
0.25 cup dill

Method
1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C.
2. Place cauliflower in food processor until smooth
3. Add parmesan and mozzarella
4. Thinly slice leek and place in bottom of roasting pan
5. Mix fish, flour and salt & pepper in bowl
6. Spoon fish mixture on top of leek
7. Spoon cauliflower mixture on top of fish
8. Cook for 20mins or until golden
9. Sprinkle with dill
