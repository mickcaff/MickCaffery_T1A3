
Ingredients
1 carrot
1 onion
2 celery stalks
1 garlic clove
0.1 kg tomato paste
0.4 kg green lentils
2 bay leaves
2 L vegetable stock
1 tbs red wine vinegar

Method
1. Heat oil in a pot. Add carrot, onion and celery. Cook 5 mins
2. Add garlic. Cook 2mins
3. Stir in tomato paste. Cook 2 mins
4. Add vegetable stock, lentils, bay leaves. Cook 20 mins or until lentils are soft
5. Add red wine vinegar and season with salt and pepper
6. Serve
