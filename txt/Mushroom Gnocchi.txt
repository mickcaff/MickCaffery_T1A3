
Ingredients
0.3 kg mushrooms
0.5 kg gnocchi
3 tbs olive oil
0.25 cup sage leaves
1 cup cream
1 garlic clove


Method
1. Heat oil in pan on medium heat
2. Fry sage leaves until crispy and put to the side
3. Add mushrooms and cook for 4 - 5 mins
4. Add cream
5. Bring to the boil for 3 - 4 mins
6. Cook the gnocchi in salted water
7. Add gnocchi to the mushroom sauce and serve with fried sage leaves
