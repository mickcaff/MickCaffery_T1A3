
Ingredients
1 cup flour
1 tsp sugar
0.5 tsp baking powder
3 carrots
0.5 kg cabbage
7 spring onions
5 eggs
1 cup vegetable stock
olive oil
kewpie mayonnaise

Method
1. In large bowl, combine flour, salt, sugar and baking powder
2. In another large bowl add thinly sliced carrot, cabbage, spring onions, and eggs.
3. Add vegetable mixture to flour mixture
4. Add vegetable stock
5. Heat oil on large fry pan on medium
6. Spoon batter onto fry pan and cook until golden brown
7. Serve
