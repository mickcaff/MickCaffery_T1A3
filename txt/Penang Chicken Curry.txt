
Ingredients
1 kg chicken thighs
2 cups pumpkin
2 tblspns vegetable oil
1/2 cup red curry paste
1 1/2 cup coconut cream
2 tblspns fish sauce
3/4 cup peanuts
1 cup thai basil 

Method
1. Oven bake pumpkin pieces 
2. Heat oil and thick top of cocunut cream in wok, until cracked
3. Add curry paste and stir until fragrant. About 5 mins
4. Add the remaining coconut cream, sugar, fish sauce and bring to the boil.
5. Add chicken pieces, simmer until meat is cooked.
6. Add basil leaves until wilted
7. Add peanuts, pumpkin pieces then serve.
