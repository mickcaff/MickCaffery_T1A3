
Ingredients
1 kg pumpkin
0.125 kg butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla essence
0.5 kg dried fruit
2 cup self raising flour

Method
1. Grease cake tin
2. Pre-heat oven to moderate
3. Boil and mash pumpkin
4. Cream the butter and sugar
5. Beat in eggs
6. Mix in vanilla essence and pumpkin
7. Add the fruit with the sifted flour
8. Pour into prepared cake tin
9. Bake for 1 hour
