
Ingredients
0.5 kg seafood marinara
0.5 cup cream
1 tbs plain flour
1 stick celery
1 onion
0.5 cup parsley
2 cup chicken stock
0.2 kg bacon
0.25 kg potatoes
0.5 tbs olive oil
1 garlic clove
0.5 cup white wine

Method
1. Fry bacon in pot
2. Add onion, celery and garlic. Cook for 4 - 5 mins
3. Stir in flour
4. Add stock and potatoes
5. Gently boil for 10 mins
6. Stir through fish & seafood. Simmer for 2 minutes.
7. Serve with parsley
